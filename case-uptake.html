Uptake — Industrial AI Platform

Turning OT signals into predictive intelligence.

Overview

At Uptake, I led product marketing and GTM for one of the most ambitious industrial AI platforms in the world — a system designed to unify operational technology (OT) data from heavy industry and convert it into predictive maintenance intelligence. The platform served oil & gas, manufacturing, energy, transportation, and water treatment operators who needed real-time visibility into asset health and performance.

Problem

Industrial operators were facing a perfect storm:

  • Fragmented OT systems across vendors and asset types
  • Unstructured sensor data with no unified schema
  • Unexpected equipment failures driving millions in downtime
  • Maintenance cycles based on guesswork instead of real conditions
  • Limited visibility into asset degradation patterns
The physical world was generating massive amounts of data — but none of it was being transformed into actionable intelligence.

Solution

I helped shape Uptake’s platform narrative and GTM strategy around a unified intelligence layer for industrial assets:

  • Unified OT data ingestion across sensors, SCADA, historians, and PLCs
  • Asset Strategy Library (ASL) — a cross‑vendor intelligence model for failure modes
  • Predictive maintenance algorithms trained on real-world industrial data
  • Anomaly detection for early warning of equipment degradation
  • Operator dashboards for asset health, risk scoring, and recommended actions
This transformed raw OT signals into a predictive, actionable intelligence layer.

Impact

The platform delivered measurable value across industries:

  • Reduced unplanned downtime across fleets, plants, and pipelines
  • Created a reusable intelligence layer for industrial assets
  • Enabled operators to shift from reactive to predictive maintenance
  • Improved asset reliability and extended equipment lifespan
  • Strengthened Uptake’s position as a leader in industrial AI
This work became a defining chapter in my Physical AI journey — proving how intelligence can transform the world’s most complex physical systems.
