UST — Retail AI & Physical AI

Bringing intelligence to the physical retail floor.

Overview

At UST, I led GTM and product marketing for a suite of AI-driven retail systems designed to connect physical store signals to digital intelligence. The goal was to transform retail environments into responsive, data-driven ecosystems capable of real-time decision-making — from inventory accuracy to customer flow optimization.

Problem

Retailers were facing a widening operational gap:

  • Inventory inaccuracies leading to stockouts and overstock
  • Slow replenishment cycles disconnected from real-time demand
  • Manual labeling and pricing workflows consuming labor hours
  • Fragmented systems between physical stores and digital platforms
  • Limited visibility into customer movement and product engagement
The physical store was rich with signals — but none of them were being captured or used intelligently.

Solution

I helped position and shape a unified Physical AI retail platform that delivered:

  • Bluetooth-based digital labeling for real-time price and product updates
  • Computer vision and sensor fusion for shelf-level inventory accuracy
  • AI-driven replenishment recommendations based on real-time demand signals
  • Customer-flow intelligence to optimize staffing and product placement
  • Unified dashboards connecting store-floor signals to enterprise systems
The result was a system that could sense, reason, and act — the core of Physical AI.

Impact

The platform delivered measurable improvements across retail operations:

  • Reduced stockouts and overstock through real-time inventory visibility
  • Improved labor efficiency by automating manual labeling and replenishment tasks
  • Increased sales through better product availability and placement
  • Connected physical store signals to digital systems for the first time
  • Enabled just-in-time retail operations across large store networks
This work became a cornerstone of my Physical AI narrative — demonstrating how intelligence can transform physical environments at scale.
